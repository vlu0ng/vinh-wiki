# Michael Finkel

American journalist and narrative nonfiction author. Contributes to *GQ*, *National Geographic*, and other major publications.

## Relevance to This Wiki

Author of *The Art Thief* (2023, Knopf), the definitive account of art thief **[[stephane-breitwieser|Stéphane Breitwieser]]**. Originally reported as "The Secrets of the World's Greatest Art Thief" in *GQ* (February 2019).

## Other Work

- *The Stranger in the Woods* (2017) — account of Christopher Knight, the "North Pond Hermit," who lived alone in the Maine woods for 27 years

## Style

Finkel works in the tradition of intimate, access-based narrative journalism — spending extended time with his subjects to understand motivation from the inside. *The Art Thief* is notable for its non-judgmental lens: Finkel presents Breitwieser's aesthetic obsession on its own terms before interrogating its ethics.
