# Gary Keller

Co-founder and chairman of Keller Williams Realty, one of the largest real estate franchise companies in the world.
Co-author of *The ONE Thing* (2013) with Jay Papasan.

## Background

Keller's central insight came from a crisis: after rapid growth, his company stalled and his personal life was in
turmoil. A coach helped him identify that there were 14 key positions that needed the right people — and that filling
those 14 roles was *the one thing* that would change everything else. He stepped down as CEO, made that his singular
focus, and the company entered a decade of 40% year-over-year growth.

## Key Contributions

- *The ONE Thing* (2013) — articulated the Focusing Question as a daily/strategic tool; built a framework for
  prioritization by elimination rather than addition
- Practical demonstration that "going small" — ruthlessly narrowing focus — produces bigger results than juggling
  many priorities
- The domino effect model of success: sequential, geometric progress beats simultaneous effort across many fronts
