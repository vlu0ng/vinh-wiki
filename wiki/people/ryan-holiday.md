# Ryan Holiday

Author and media strategist who became one of the most prominent popularizers of Stoic philosophy. Former director
of marketing at American Apparel; left to focus on writing after Stoicism reshaped his understanding of what a good
life looks like.

## Intellectual Evolution

Holiday's early engagement with Stoicism was self-serving — discipline, resilience, emotional control as
competitive tools. His writing has evolved toward a more other-directed, socially conscious reading. He describes this
as a natural arc: the Stoics who endured history did so through service, not self-mastery alone.

## Stoic Virtues Trilogy

1. *The Obstacle Is the Way* (2014) — courage and resilience; turning adversity into advantage
2. *Ego Is the Enemy* (2016) — temperance; the dangers of arrogance and self-absorption
3. *Right Thing, Right Now* (2023) — *justice*; the social dimension of character

## Key Contributions

- Brought Marcus Aurelius, Seneca, and Epictetus to a modern popular audience
- Framed Stoicism not as passive resignation but as an action-orienting philosophy for leaders
- *Right Thing, Right Now* recenters ethics on other-directedness: truth-telling, self-accountability, fairness,
  and service to the common good
- The Truman case study: showed how a seemingly ordinary person (failed businessman, modest credentials) built
  extraordinary leadership capacity through consistent virtue practice over decades

## Key Works
- The Daily Stoic series; *Stillness Is the Key*; *Lives of the Stoics*; *Discipline Is Destiny*
